About Me

A native of Kansas, I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Engineering from Kansas State University in 1995. I moved to Austin shortly thereafter. I have worked in the Semiconductor industry for 18 years. I have coded in a dozen langagues, done circuit design, PCB design and dabbled in mechanical design with Sketchup.

I have travelled to China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Taiwan, France, Italy, Spain, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Canada, Mexico and Czechia. I have spent the most time in Taiwan and Hong Kong, having spent a few months there.

I am an Electric Vehicle promoter, drive a Tesla model X and foster kittens!
